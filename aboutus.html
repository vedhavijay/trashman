About Us

Chennai’s first App based doorstep recycling based platform developed by mechanical engineers for all household and commericial customers

TEAMTRASHMAN

Trashman is Chennai’s first App based Waste Management service that provides a free doorstep pick-up service for households, offices, institutions and schools to dispose their recyclable waste like Newspaper, Iron, Plastics, Books, Copies, Metals, Home-Appliances etc and get paid for it.

As an initial stage Trashman service is started in Tambaram, Mudichur, Chrompet, Hastinapuram, Selaiyur, Aalapakkam, Perungalathur, Perumbakkam and Sholinganallur. We are trying to expand, as soon as possible throughout chennai.
